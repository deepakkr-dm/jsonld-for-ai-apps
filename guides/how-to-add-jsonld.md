# How to Add JSON-LD to Your Website

## Step 1
Copy the schema JSON

## Step 2
Wrap it inside:

<script type="application/ld+json">
...your JSON...
</script>

## Step 3
Paste inside your HTML page (head or body)

## Step 4
Validate using:
https://search.google.com/test/rich-results

---

## Common mistakes

- Invalid JSON format  
- Missing quotes  
- Wrong schema type  
- Duplicate schema on page  

---

## Where to use

- AI product pages  
- SaaS landing pages  
- Blog posts
